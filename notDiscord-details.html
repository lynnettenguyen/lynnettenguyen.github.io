notDiscord

  • Frontend: React, Redux, HTML5, CSS3
  • Backend: SocketIO, Flask, SQLAlchemy
  • Project Date: August 2022
  • Live Site: not-discord-app.herokuapp.com

notDiscord is a messaging application based on Discord. notDiscord utilizes SocketIO to maintain Websocket connections for live chat and RESTful APIs for intuitive access to server channels and direct messages.
